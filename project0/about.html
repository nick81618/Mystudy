About Me

I am a young Togolese full of adventure that had for the first time set foot in china in April 2013. I am now a young entrepreneur defying many different fields from been a computer science student graduate to been the founder of three different structures in China. In 2017 I founded ROOTS ASSOCIATION based in Nanjing City that has for main objective to introduce the African Culture the Chinese and Foreign community in China. In December 2018 I have launched with the help of my girlfriend BORNBLACK CLOTHING AND ACCESSORIES BRAND which first phase has consisted in registering BornBlack Brand in 28 different countries in Africa including China. BornBlack Brand has officially seen light in May 2019 when the first mobile app was published. In July 2019 my Third structure was created, NANJING FAADEM MANAGEMENT & CONSULTING CO., LTD. Nanjing Faadem is a company that aim for the wellbeing of Africa and for a start my Country, Togo for which I am working hard to help my Government in terms of bringing investments to the country and slowly raise the employment rate in Togo.
